On May 5th, 2009, I lost my boyfriend to suicide. After years of reviewing studies, I discovered that Asian Americans were significantly less likely to seek help from mental health professionals because of the many cultural stigmas surrounding mental health and suicide. I became inspired to create Support with Love in memory of him; for those who are dealing with stigmas of mental health so that they have a safe community to reach out and receive support.

"Too often we underestimate the power of a touch, a smile, a kind word, a listening ear, an honest compliment, or the smallest act of caring, all of which have the potential to turn a life around." -Leo Buscaglia
